## 🌐 Web 3.0 Basics

Web 3.0 is the next generation of the internet, emphasizing:

* **Decentralization**
* **Blockchain-based systems**
* **User ownership of data and digital identity**

---

## 📘 Key Concepts

* **Blockchain**: A decentralized digital ledger that records transactions transparently and immutably.
* **DeFi (Decentralized Finance)**: Financial services built on blockchain that eliminate the need for centralized intermediaries like banks.
* **NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens)**: Unique digital assets representing ownership of artwork, collectibles, or real-world items.
* **Smart Contracts**: Self-executing agreements coded on the blockchain.
* **DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations)**: Community-driven organizations managed through transparent voting and smart contracts.

---

## 🔗 Popular Blockchain Networks

* **Ethereum**: The most widely used smart contract platform.
* **Polygon**: A Layer 2 solution that scales Ethereum with lower fees.
* **Solana**: Known for its high-speed and low-cost blockchain transactions.
* **Binance Smart Chain (BSC)**: A fast, Ethereum-compatible blockchain powered by Binance.

---

## 💬 Common Web3 Terms

* **Gas Fees**: Fees paid to process blockchain transactions.
* **Wallet**: A digital application (like MetaMask) for storing and managing crypto assets.
* **Mining / Staking**: Processes for securing blockchain networks and earning rewards.
* **Yield Farming**: Earning crypto by lending or staking digital assets in DeFi platforms.

---

## 🛡️ Security Best Practices

✅ Never share your **private keys** or **seed phrases**
✅ Use **hardware wallets** for storing significant funds
✅ Always verify **smart contract addresses** before interacting
✅ Stay alert to **phishing links or scam projects**
✅ Research and **DYOR (Do Your Own Research)** before investing
